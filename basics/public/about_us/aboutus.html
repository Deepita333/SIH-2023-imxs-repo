OVERVIEW

Welcome to our specialized mental health therapy website dedicated to supporting students and individuals within the Deaf and Mute communities. At SAATHI, we understand that the pursuit of education can be both exciting and challenging, and that unique communication needs can impact one's mental health journey. That's why we are here to offer compassionate and tailored therapeutic support.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide accessible, empathetic, and effective mental health services to students and those within the Deaf and Mute communities. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to thrive academically and emotionally, regardless of their communication abilities or barriers. Our practice is designed to be fully inclusive, providing specialized support for those who communicate through sign language, writing, or other non-verbal means. We customize therapy plans to address your specific needs and goals, ensuring a personalized and effective therapeutic journey. We understand the importance of convenient access to mental health services. Our online therapy options ensure that you can receive support from the comfort of your own environment. Our therapists are skilled in sign language, communication alternatives, and culturally relevant approaches. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. We adhere to strict confidentiality standards to ensure your trust and comfort.
